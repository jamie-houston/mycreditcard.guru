📚 External Resources & Guides

Learn from the experts. We recommend these high-quality external blogs, websites, and community spaces to help you master credit card rewards and point redemptions.

doctorofcredit.com
Beginner No Affiliate Links

Widely regarded as the most reliable, objective, and unbiased credit card news site. Doctor of Credit does not use credit card affiliate referral links, meaning their recommendations are entirely free of financial bias.

star Best For: Real-time updates on the highest signup bonuses, bank promotions, and leak alerts.
flight_takeoff Frequent Miler
frequentmiler.com
Intermediate Advanced

An excellent blog that covers points and miles optimization with high integrity. They provide deep-dive analyses, spreadsheet-backed comparisons, and strategy guides for complex loyalty programs and airline transfer partners.

star Best For: Points valuations, transfer partner strategies, sweet spots, and automated tracking tools.
explore The Points Guy
thepointsguy.com
Beginner

The largest and most mainstream points and miles publication on the web. While heavily monetized via affiliate links, it remains a fantastic starting point for beginners to read about credit card basics, lounge guides, and reviews.

star Best For: Beginner tutorial series, airport lounge guides, card news, and hotel/airline reviews.
forum Reddit: r/CreditCards
reddit.com/r/CreditCards
Beginner Intermediate

A friendly, community-run forum with hundreds of thousands of members discussing everyday credit card strategies. Users frequently share recommendations, setup designs (e.g. Chase Trifecta vs. Amex Quadfecta), and application results.

star Best For: Getting answers to specific card questions and browsing community recommendations.
psychology Reddit: r/churning
reddit.com/r/churning
Advanced

A highly structured, fast-paced community for advanced users focused strictly on maximizing credit card signup bonuses. Warning: The community has strict rules and a low tolerance for basic questions, so be sure to read their wiki and sidebar before posting.

star Best For: Detailed application flowcharts, velocity rules, and advanced methods for meeting signup minimum spends.
inventory AwardWallet & Point.me
awardwallet.com | point.me
Beginner Intermediate

Utility services that help you manage and redeem the points you earn. AwardWallet tracks your loyalty account balances and expiration dates, while Point.me acts as a search engine for finding award flight seat availability.

star Best For: Tracking points balances, flight search engines, and preventing point expiration.
